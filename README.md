# HR Analytics Dashboard

## 1. Project Title / Headline

**HR Analytics Dashboard**

An interactive Power BI dashboard developed to analyze employee data and provide insights into workforce performance, employee attrition, job roles, salary distribution, promotions, and departmental trends.

---

## 2. Short Description / Purpose

The HR Analytics Dashboard is an interactive Power BI report designed to help HR professionals and business leaders monitor workforce metrics and identify factors influencing employee attrition. It provides valuable insights into employee demographics, job roles, salaries, promotions, and department-wise performance to support strategic HR decision-making.

---

## 3. Tech Stack

The dashboard was built using the following tools and technologies:

- 📊 **Power BI Desktop** – Main data visualization platform used for dashboard development.
- 📂 **Power Query** – Used for data cleaning, transformation, and preparation.
- 🧠 **DAX (Data Analysis Expressions)** – Used for creating calculated measures, KPIs, and dynamic reports.
- 🔗 **Data Modeling** – Established relationships between employee and HR datasets.
- 📈 **Interactive Visualizations** – KPI Cards, Bar Charts, Pie Charts, Column Charts, and Slicers.
- 📁 **File Format** – `.pbix` for development and `.png` for dashboard preview.

---

## 4. Data Source

**Source:** HR Employee Attrition Dataset

The dataset contains approximately **10,000 employee records**, including:

- Employee Details
- Department
- Job Role
- Gender
- Marital Status
- Salary Information
- Promotion History
- Work-Life Balance
- Attrition Status
- Working Years

The data was cleaned, transformed, and modeled using Power Query before creating the dashboard.

---

## 5. Features / Highlights

### • Business Problem

Organizations often struggle to identify the factors contributing to employee attrition and workforce performance. Questions such as:

- Which departments experience the highest attrition?
- Which job roles are most affected?
- How does salary influence attrition?
- Does work-life balance impact employee retention?
- How do promotions relate to employee attrition?

are difficult to answer using raw HR data.

### • Goal of the Dashboard

The dashboard was developed to:

- Monitor key HR KPIs.
- Analyze employee attrition trends.
- Evaluate department and job role performance.
- Understand salary and promotion impacts.
- Support data-driven workforce planning.

### • Walkthrough of Key Visuals

#### 📌 Key KPIs

- Total Employees
- Attrition Rate
- Average Hourly Rate
- Average Working Years

#### 💼 Job Role vs Work-Life Balance

Compares employee count and work-life balance across different job roles.

#### 🏢 Attrition Rate by Department

Displays attrition distribution across various departments.

#### 📈 Promotion vs Attrition

Analyzes the relationship between employee promotions and attrition.

#### 💰 Salary Slab vs Attrition

Shows employee attrition across different salary ranges.

#### 🎛 Interactive Filters

Users can filter the dashboard using:

- Department
- Job Role
- Gender
- Marital Status
- Salary Slab

### • Business Impact & Insights

- Identified departments with higher employee attrition.
- Evaluated the impact of salary on employee retention.
- Analyzed work-life balance across job roles.
- Monitored promotion trends and their relationship with attrition.
- Enabled HR teams to make informed workforce planning and retention decisions.
